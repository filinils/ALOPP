Alfa Laval: the retrofit experts

Experience goes a long way. We use our hands-on knowledge to adapt the Alfa Laval PureBallast 3.1 to serve your needs. Its compact design fits any space. And Alfa Laval’s proven working procedures help you maximize efficiency. We customize your retrofit to your vessel, equipment and schedule. And our service is second to none.

Alfa Laval Partnerships

Flexibility and co-operation across the globe.

Efficient retrofits rely on successful partnerships, so co-operation and flexibility drive Alfa Laval partnerships both globally and locally.

You choose whether we support your engineering teams or provide experts from our global network.

We offer

  • Installation during docking or on the move
  • Flexible schedules and location if your trading routes change
  • Adaptability to major changes mid-project
  • Engineering, 3D laser scanning and class approval support
